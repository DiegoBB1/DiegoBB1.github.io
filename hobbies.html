Hobbies

When I'm not practicing coding problems or working on new projects, I enjoy staying active, exploring new places, and unwinding time with some of my favorite hobbies.

Running has been one of my go-to ways to clear my mind and stay energized ever since high school, and it's a habit that keeps me grounded and focused. I also enjoy playing video games every now and then, which has always been a great way to unwind and relax.

I'm also a lifelong Houston sports fan, with a particular love for football and baseball. Houston has always felt like a second home to me, as I spent much of my childhood visiting there and watching Texans games with my family. Supporting Houston teams, especially the Texans and Astros, brings back a lot of those memories and keeps me connected to a city that's close to my heart.
